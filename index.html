Muneya Commands



Commands

  1. mu!help - Provides a list of commands.
  2. mu!stats - Provides Muneya's Stats
  3. mu!me - Provides you with Basic info about you.
  4. mu!bruh - Reacts with emojis that spell bruh.
  5. mu!spotify - Reacts with Spotify emoji.
  6. mu!simp - Reacts with Simp Alert.
  7. mu!ghost - Reacts with a Ghost.
  8. mu!upgrade - Tells you about MuneyaEnterprise.
  9. mu!history - Tells you about our history.
  10. mu!vote - Takes you to Muneya's vote page.
  11. mu!bugs - Takes you to Bug Catching form.
  12. mu!github - Takes you to our Github page.
  13. mu!donate - Takes you to Muneya's donation page.
  14. mu!hello - Starts a DM w/ you.
  15. mu!music lofi - Plays LofiMusic in your VC.
  16. mu!music kawaii - Plays KawaiiMusic in your VC.
  17. mu!music thot - Plays Number One Pillar in your VC.
